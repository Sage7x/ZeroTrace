# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in ZeroTrace should be respectful, technically honest, and focused on improving the project.

Expected behavior:

1. discuss ideas and code directly without personal attacks
2. assume good intent, but be precise about technical disagreement
3. respect security-sensitive information and disclosure boundaries
4. keep reviews focused on the work, not the contributor
5. welcome contributors with different levels of experience

Unacceptable behavior:

1. harassment or intimidation
2. discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
3. doxxing or sharing private information without consent
4. bad-faith security disclosure
5. repeated disruptive behavior after clear feedback

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate discussions, close inappropriate threads, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its community.
